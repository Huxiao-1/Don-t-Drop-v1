Don't Drop

Don't Drop! 一款轻量级多人派对乱斗游戏，极简操作，秒开即玩！支持 4 人同屏欢乐对战，主打轻松同乐、非竞技对抗。玩家在平台上奔跑、跳跃、攻击，把对手撞下平台即得分，每人 3 条生命，坚持到最后就是赢家。对战中随机掉落加速、护盾、炸弹等道具，充满惊喜与变数，超适合聚会开黑、轻松解压！ A lightweight multiplayer party brawler with ultra-simplified controls for instant fun! It supports 4-player local battles and focuses on shared joy rather than fierce competition. Players run, jump and attack on platforms to knock opponents off; each player has 3 lives, and the last one standing wins. Random power-ups like speed boost, shield and bomb drop during matches, bringing constant surprises. Perfect for parties and casual fun!
